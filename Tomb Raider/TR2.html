Tomb Raider 2

Tomb Raider 2 is the successor of Tomb Raider 1, released in 1997. It follows the adventures of Lara Croft as she explores ancient tombs and uncovers secrets.


Gameplay Features

Exploration of ancient tombs

Puzzle-solving elements

Combat with various enemies

Platforming challenges

Iconic character: Lara Croft

Other characters: Marco Bartoli, Chan Barkhang, Fabio (Driver of Marco's Airplane), Eros

See here charatchers list

Weapons: Pistols, Shotgun, Automatic Pistols, Uzis, M16, Grenade Launcher, Harpoon Gun

Enemies: Tigers, T-rex, Spiders, Velociraptors, a T-Rex, Lions, Crocodriles, Gorillas, Mummies, Panthers, Atlantaen Mutants, Atlantaen Centaurs

Story

About 500 years ago at Los Alamos in New Mexico a Meteorite felt there, and made free a capsule that contains inside a creature, came from lost city of Atliantis. Once free her decided to relive the life, so she is the boss of the Natla Techonologies. A day sent one of her workers, Larson, to Lara Croft for offering her to search a piece of an Artifact called Scion, with umbelievle powers.
