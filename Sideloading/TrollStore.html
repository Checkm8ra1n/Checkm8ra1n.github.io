TrollStore

TrollStore is an iPA installer for iOS 16.6.1 and below that uses a CoreTrust Bug

To install TrollStore, your deice must be at iOS 17.0 or lower (iOS 16.7 and higher not supported)

Now to begin the TrollStore installation, you have to install TrollInstallerX with Sideloadly or AltStore, so follow my guides for use the other sideloading tools; just click the button below for download TrollInstallerX iPA


Download TrollInstallerX

After you have installed TrollInstallerX, you will find TrollStore on your homescreen
