TrollStore

Requirements

  • A device with iOS 16.5 or earlier or iOS 17.0 (not iOS 17.1 or later)
  • A Sideloading tool, see above
  • TrollInstallerX

Usage

  • Install TrollInstallerX with a Sideloading tool
  • Now open TrollInstallerX and click on install TrollStore
  • Once finished to install TrollStore, you can find it on your home screen.
