AltStore

Requirements

  • A Computer with Windows or macOS
  • AltServer
  • USB cable or Wifi connection (not 5G)

Installation

  • Download and Install AltServer from the button above.
  • Connect your iOS device to your computer or make sure to be on the same Wi-Fi network.
  • Open AltServer, you will find it on the top menu (macOS) or in the system tray (Windows).
    1. Select install AltStore and your iOS device.
    2. Enter your Apple ID and Password
    3. All Done, you should see AltStore on your Home Screen

Install Apps

  • Open AltStore, make sure that AltServer is opened on your PC and your iPhone is connected via USB or same Wifi
  • Click on My Apps, on the Plus Button and choose the iPA you wish to install
  • You will be prompted to enter your Apple ID and Password, you have to wait a few minutes for the installation to complete
