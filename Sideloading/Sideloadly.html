Sideloadly

Requirements

  • A Computer with Windows or macOS
  • Sideloadly (macOS)

    Sideloadly (Windows)
  • USB cable or Wifi connection (not 5G)

Usage

  • Download Sideloadly for your OS, install it through the install (Windows) or dmg (macOS)
  • Now open Sideloadly, connect your iOS device to your computer or connect it to the same wifi network
  • Now on the Apple ID text field, enter your Apple ID
  • Now choose the iPA you wish to install, click on install, enter your Apple ID Password, now you should see the app installing on your device
